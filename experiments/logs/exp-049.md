## EXP-049: num_clusters × h × embedding model sweep (fixes EXP-045 bug)

[English](exp-049.md) | [Chinese](exp-049.zh.md)

**Date**: 2026-04-30
**Status**: completed

### Background
EXP-045 Found num_clusters=1024 bug (should be 4096), all results are not trustworthy. This experiment is rerun with the correct configuration and the scan range is expanded:
- num_clusters: 4096 vs 8192 (uniform) vs MGMR (8192×4096, 8192×2048, 4096×2048)
- FSQ hidden dim h: 64 vs 128
- Embedding model: 0.6B vs 4B

### Design

14 variants = 2 embedding models × (4 nc configs × 2 h + 3 MGMR configs):

| Type | Variants |
|------|---------|
| 0.6b uniform | nc4096-h64, nc4096-h128, nc8192-h64, nc8192-h128 |
| 4b uniform | nc4096-h64, nc4096-h128, nc8192-h64, nc8192-h128 |
| 0.6b MGMR | nc8192x4096-h128, nc8192x2048-h128, nc4096x2048-h128 |
| 4b MGMR | nc8192x4096-h128, nc8192x2048-h128, nc4096x2048-h128 |

### Analysis

1. **nc=8192 is the decisive variable**: Gini_d2 dropped from 0.35 to 0.24 (0.6b), snHR increased from 0.080 to 0.092, codebook utilization and neighbor quality were greatly improved.
2. **h=64 vs h=128 No difference**: Gini/snHR is exactly the same, but CR is slightly lower. h=128 is recommended as standard.
3. **MGMR (hierarchical nc) is overall worse than uniform nc=8192**: snHR is consistent with Gini_d2, performance decreases after L2 is reduced (0.6b: 0.092→0.089/0.084), hierarchical compression is unhelpful.
4. **4b is better than 0.6b** across the board: snHR 0.131 vs 0.092, the difference is significant. 4b embedding has a semantic neighbor retention rate of 42%, which is a better basis for tokenizer.
5. **Gini_d2 has the same direction as snHR**: Both are optimal at nc=8192 uniform and verify each other. snHR as an independent FORGE proxy metric is fully consistent with the Gini_d2 conclusion.
