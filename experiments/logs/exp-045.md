### Background
EXP-043 analysis (Analysis #4) points out that the collision rate of 4B and 8B SID is too high (2.76% / 5.44%),
The root cause is that `fsq_mlp_hidden=64` is designed for 0.6B (dim=1024) and for 4B (dim=2560) and 8B (dim=4096)
It is a serious bottleneck, causing MLP to be unable to fully distinguish the high-dimensional embedding space, and a large number of items are mapped to the same SID.

### Hypothesis
Expanding fsq_mlp_hidden to dim/10 (4B→256, 8B→512) can:
1. Significantly reduce collision rate (4B: 2.76%→<1%, 8B: 5.44%→<2%)
2. Improve R@10 (currently suppressed by collision)
3. 4B SID is expected to reach or exceed the R@10 of 0.6B SID

### Design
- **Fixed**: 12d_4096 FSQ levels, mlp projection, S-tier NTP, 14d data, full features
- **0.6b does not rerun**: collision=0.49% is acceptable, directly quote exp043-s-0.6b

| exp045-4b-h256 | Qwen3-4B | 64 | 256 | exp045-4b-h256 | S-tier |
| exp045-8b-h512 | Qwen3-8B | 64 | 512 | exp045-8b-h512 | S-tier |
