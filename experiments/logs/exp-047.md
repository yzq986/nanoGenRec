## EXP-047: L-tier NTP — All Validated Optimizations (scale-07, 101M active)
[English](exp-047.md) | [Chinese](exp-047.zh.md)


**Date**: 2026-04-30
**Status**: completed

### Background
The S-tier experimental chain (EXP-043~046) verified a set of optimized combinations: TO-RoPE ts=0.5 (+2.7pp), gate_attn (+0.4pp), segment_emb + time_gap + action_level. M-tier (71.6M active) reaches R@500=70.2% in EXP-043.

This experiment transplants all verified optimizations to L-tier (scale-07, 101.1M active) as the starting point of SFT for the RL link.

### Design
- **Model**: scale-07 (embed_dim=512, 12L, 16E top-2 MoE, 101.1M active)
- **Features**: segment_emb + time_gap + action_level + TO-RoPE (order:0.5, time:0.5) + gate_attn
- **Data**: exp044b-0.6b-14d; SID: exp026-0.6b-14d; LR: 2e-4

### Results

| Config | R@10 | R@500 | PPL | Wall |
|--------|------|-------|-----|------|
| exp043-m-0.6b (M-tier bare, ref) | 14.5% | 70.2% | 18.54 | 23min |
| **exp047 (L-tier + all opts)** | **12.8%** | **64.1%** | **20.7** | **42min** |

### Analysis

1. **R@500 64.1%, lower than expected (72-74%)**: L-tier (101M active) has 40% more parameters than M-tier (71.6M active), but only 1.3 tok/param in 1 epoch, which is seriously under-trained.
2. **PPL=20.7 is higher than M-tier bare (18.54)**: A larger model will fit worse under the same amount of data, which is a reflection of under-training.
3. **Conclusion**: L-tier single epoch is inferior to M-tier. Taking advantage requires ≥3 epochs or more data. The RL link still starts at exp047 (R@500=64.1% > S-tier SFT threshold).
